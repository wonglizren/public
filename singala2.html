what the sutta actually says

Four friends.
Sixteen small signs.

The sutta names four good-hearted friends, and gives each one four ways to be recognised — sixteen signs in all, drawn from watching how a person actually behaves.

Everything on the next screen is straight from the text. Nothing added, nothing modernised — that part comes later, and it'll be your job, not ours.

Open questions.

The Good Counsel restrains you from doing wrong. Has a friend ever stopped you from something — and were you grateful then, or only later?

2

3
